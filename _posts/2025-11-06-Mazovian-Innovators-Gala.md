Last week we had pleasure of participate in the Gala of Mazovian
Innovator Contest. There were two categories: Innovative Company,
Innovative Scientist. Innovation as you will see have many meanings,
that are sometimes difficult to comprehend.  
Photin submission as innovative company was:  
*Technology of compound semiconductors Indium Arsenide MOCVD growth
demonstrated with two products*:

1.  Magnetoresistive sensors grown for the Denmark Technical University
    purchased by dr Thierry Desire Pomar who wrote: “Extraordinary
    magnetometry: A review on extraordinary magnetoresistance”
    <https://doi.org/10.1016/j.apmt.2024.102219>

2.  Mid-infrared detectors in the 2-3.5µm range made of Indium Arsenide,
    and InAsSbP compound. Photin wafers were taken by Military
    University of Technology scientists, and processed into MWIR
    detectors.  
    Those MWIR detectors parameters exceeded the catalog values of
    commercial American, Japanese, Russian and Polish manufacturers.

Photin compound semiconductor MOCVD technology and products, must have
provide a lot of brain ache to experts and judging panel, as they need
to acquire some semiconductor knowledge to judge application.  
We are very happy to widespread a little information about our
semiconductor remarkable developments to wider audience. This year our
innovation do not qualified for main prizes, but we were rewarded with
distinction!

The 3 winners SME with cash prize are following:

1.  30k PLN RDLS sp. Z O.O.: “Natural Wastewater Treatment Plants that
    operate on the basis of passive hydraulics and subsurface flow”.
    <https://rdls.pl/>

2.  20k PLN Polblat Kopyt sp. J.: “New technology for the production of
    technological countertops for the production of concrete products by
    vibropressing”. <https://polblat.pl/>

3.  15k PLN Baktrem sp. Z O.O.: “The technology of disposal of wood
    waste soaked in creosote oil”. <https://bactrem.pl/>
