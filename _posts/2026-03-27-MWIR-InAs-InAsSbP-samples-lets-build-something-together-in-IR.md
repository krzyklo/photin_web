We were looking for easy way to protect our samples of MWIR InAs
detectors chips for shipment to customer, so they would be well
protected, couldn’t be mixed up, take least amount of space, and be
inexpensive.  
During brainstorming, we “stepped on something”, which turned out to be
perfect package for small batch of semiconductor chips.  
Lets build something together in infrared.

**\#Made in Poland, \#Made in EU**
